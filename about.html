Hotel Facilities

Restaurant

Savor exquisite cuisine in our on-site restaurant, offering a diverse menu of locally inspired dishes and international favorites. Whether you're enjoying a casual meal or a special celebration, our warm ambiance and impeccable service ensure a memorable dining experience.

Swimming Pool

Take a dip in our outdoor swimming pool, offering a relaxing oasis for both leisurely swims and sunbathing. Enjoy the refreshing waters and poolside seating as you unwind and enjoy the surrounding views.

Fitness Area

Maintain your workout routine in our fully equipped fitness center, featuring modern cardio machines, free weights, and a variety of exercise equipment. Open 24 hours for your convenience.

Mini Bar

Each room is equipped with a well-stocked minibar featuring a selection of beverages, snacks, and premium refreshments. Perfect for enjoying a quiet night in or a refreshing drink after a long day.

Meeting Room

For business travelers, our fully-equipped meeting room is available, featuring high-speed internet, presentation equipment, and comfortable seating arrangements. Whether hosting a small meeting or a larger conference, our space provides the perfect environment for productivity.

Laundry Service

Our on-site laundry room offers self-service laundry facilities, allowing you to freshen up your clothes during your stay. We also offer dry cleaning services for those needing extra convenience.

Satelite TV

Stay entertained with a range of international and local channels available through our state-of-the-art satellite TV service. Whether you’re unwinding after a busy day or enjoying a movie night in, there’s something for everyone.

Safe Box

For your peace of mind, each room comes with a personal safe box to securely store your valuables, ensuring that you can relax and enjoy your stay without worry.

Parking Area

Enjoy the convenience of our secure parking area, available for guests with private vehicles. We offer both outdoor and covered parking spaces to ensure your vehicle is safe during your stay.
